Controls:
 - Camera move: WASD.
 - Camera look: Click drag to start, click to exit.
 - Highlight surface: Mouse over surface.
 - Select surfaces: Click surface, shift-click-drag for box select, shift-click surface to add.
 - Lock surface with camera move: Click hold surface and move.
 - Move surfaces in screen: Click drag surface.
 - Edit surfaces: Double click surface.
 - Rotate surfaces: Surface rotate handles.
